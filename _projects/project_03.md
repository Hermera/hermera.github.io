We regard the neural network as a black box and study similarity of networks through the performance in the two downstream tasks.

We used transfer learning to explore how differently-initialized neural networks show different performances. 

- ![](/images/projects/03.png)
- The accuracy of five differently-initialized networks (transfer learning from Cifar10)


And we used adversarial attack to compute the decision boundary(the intensity of the attack that can successfully attack the network) and measure the similarity of neural networks.

- ![](/images/projects/02.png)
- Visualization of decision boundaries


More details can be found in this [Chinese version report](/files/Machine_Learning_Term_Project.pdf).
